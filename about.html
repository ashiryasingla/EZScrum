🌟 About EZScrum

EZScrum was created to make Scrum project management easy and accessible. In a world full of overly complex tools, EZScrum focuses only on what teams truly need: managing backlogs and sprints efficiently. No account required. Organize tasks, assign work, and track progress with our intuitive checklist system.
